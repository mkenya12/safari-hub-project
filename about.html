About Safarihub

Simplifying Nairobi's transport since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Safarihub was born out of frustration with Nairobi's complex transport system. As daily commuters ourselves, we experienced firsthand the challenges of navigating the city's matatus, shuttles, and buses.

What started as a simple spreadsheet shared among friends has grown into Nairobi's most trusted transport guide. Today, we serve over 10,000 daily users, helping them save time, money, and stress on their daily commutes.

Our mission is simple: to make Nairobi's transport system accessible, understandable, and easy to use for everyone.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, real-time transport information that empowers Nairobi residents to make smarter commuting decisions, saving them time and reducing the stress of daily travel.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading transport information platform, transforming how people navigate cities through technology and community-driven information.

Our Values

Accuracy

We verify all information through multiple sources and user reports to ensure you get reliable data.

Community

We believe in the power of community. Your reports and feedback help others navigate better.

Innovation

Constantly improving our platform with new features like AI route suggestions and real-time crowdedness updates.

Accessibility

Making transport information available to everyone, regardless of technical knowledge or physical ability.
